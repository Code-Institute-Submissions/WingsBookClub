About Series

I absolutely adore reading series, don't you? When characters take hold of your imagination and don't want to let go, it's always nice to know the next book is already waiting to carry on telling you their story. It's like catching up on gossip with a close friend!

So that's why in this section, I'll be listing my favourite series. Just move your mouse over or tap on the image for a summary.

The Chocolate Lovers' Club

by Carole Matthews

You know that friend who always seems to attract misfortune and just won't listen to advice? That's our chief chocoholic, Lucy.

Between stops at her favourite chocolate shop, aptly named Chocolate Heaven, she juggles an unfaithful boyfriend, a challenging, ever-changing career in temping and helps out her dear friends and fellow members of the Chocolate Lovers' Club.
