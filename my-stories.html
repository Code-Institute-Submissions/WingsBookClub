About My Stories

You want to read my writings? Aww, shucks, thank you! Hope you enjoy them and feel free to drop me a line to tell me what you think of them.

Now, as you can see, most of these are fan fiction. I discovered fan fiction at a time in my life that was really difficult, being bullied at school pre-diagnosis as well as some troubles at home. Fan fiction saved my life, and you can take that literally. Through writing stories, I found I could get out the emotions safely as well as get someone - even a fictional character - to say the words I needed to hear. Many of those old stories are therefore a bit on the dark side; I will add warnings to individual stories if that is the case.

Happy Reading!
